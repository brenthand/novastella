Services

How can we help?

Translations

NovaStella translators are seasoned, qualified, professional translators who are ready to help you with all your translation needs. Our translators are well acquainted with the diverse cultures and markets for which they translate. We can provide you with a prompt and accurate translation from a qualified native speaker of the target language. We are experienced, professional translators who will help you say what you really mean.

Proofreading & Editing

If you already have a document in a foreign language, but just want to make sure that it is well written and conveys the right message, we can review it and provide comprehensive feedback. Our trusted and experienced proofreaders prepare documents using the "track changes" function within Microsoft Word, which allows you to move easily between changes, and accept any or all changes proposed.

Website Translation

As your business expands to global markets, so will your website. NovaStella can translate your website to fit the cultural language of your targeted countries. We can take your existing site, prepare a translation document, and then have this translated, making sure our native speaking translators constantly refer to the original website to make sure the translation is always done in context.
